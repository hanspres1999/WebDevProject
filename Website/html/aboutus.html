Need for this System


“I once had fever and had to go visit the doctor’s clinic. The doctor was the best in the area and patients flocked to visit him. An important observation that I made was that the doctor never had any sort on online resource or any sort of software to help in his routine work. That seemed to intrigue me and as a computer engineer in the process with an inspiration in the form of a Subject call Web Technologies, I and my team took it upon ourselves to make a Web application fashion some sort of help for not only our doctor but every general physician who may require it. This is the main purpose and inspiration behind developing this web application”
